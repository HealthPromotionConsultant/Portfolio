My approach

Warm, structured facilitation that is culturally safe, trauma-informed, and built for action.

Trauma‑informed facilitation

I prioritize psychological safety, consent-based participation, and strategies that don’t require people to “push through.”

  • Choice-based activities
  • Non-judgmental language
  • Grounding + regulation tools

Culturally safe delivery

I adapt content to community context and lived experience—without assumptions and without blame.

  • Culture-aware examples
  • Respectful discussion norms
  • Anti-oppression lens

Accessibility matters

Sessions are designed for mixed literacy and ESL contexts. I use clear visuals, short steps, repetition, and gentle pacing. Interpreter-friendly delivery is available.
